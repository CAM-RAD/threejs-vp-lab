## What It Does

InterPositive works WITHIN existing production pipelines. it does NOT generate environments from scratch (unlike Acme AI / Killing Satoshi). it builds an AI model from a production's dailies (the footage already shot) and uses that model in post for:

- Color grading
- Relighting
- Continuity fixes
- Wire removal
- Reframing
- Background enhancement

Ben Affleck: "It's not about text-prompting or generating something from nothing."

## How It Works

The system teaches an AI model to understand HOW a shot was filmed: focal length, camera movement, framing style, subject distance. then it applies that understanding to automate post-production tasks that currently require manual VFX work.

## Cost Savings Claimed

- VFX costs: 50% reduction
- Background actors / stand-ins: 70% savings
- Overall below-the-line production: 10-20% savings (conservative estimate)

## Relevance to Our Studio

InterPositive's approach can be layered on top of ANY production method — LED volume, gray box, or traditional location work. it's a post-production efficiency tool. the gray box approach is a production methodology. they're complementary, not competing.
